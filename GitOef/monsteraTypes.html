Monstera Deliciosa

The Monstera Deliciosa, often called the Swiss Cheese Plant, is a popular tropical houseplant known for its large, glossy leaves with natural holes and splits.

Care Tips

  • Bright, indirect light
  • Water when the top soil is dry
  • Moderate humidity preferred
  • Avoid direct harsh sunlight

Fun Fact

In the wild, Monstera plants climb trees using aerial roots and can grow extremely large leaves.
